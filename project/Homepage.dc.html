From Conversation to Implementation

The Leverage Kickstart process, in three stages.

1

Discover

A complimentary session where we learn about your business, your goals, and the challenges you're trying to solve. By the end, you'll have clarity on your biggest opportunities, and we'll know which Leverage Kickstart option is right for you.

  • A 45 to 60 minute conversation about your business
  • A detailed Discover Summary report
  • Clear guidance on the next step
2

Leverage Summary

We map what we learned against your current systems, tools, and workflows and hand you a clear diagnosis: what to build, simplify, or automate first, in the order that creates the most leverage.

  • A prioritized list of projects
  • A scope of work to be completed
  • Put us to work
3

Leverage Kickstart

We work alongside your team to build the automations, SOPs, and tech stack changes from your Summary, then test them until they run the way they're supposed to.

  • Full project implementation
  • Clear communication throughout
  • Training on how to get the most from what we built
